Music Slide Show Maker

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Music Slide Show Maker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Point: Music Slide Show Maker is designed with privacy in mind. All your photos, videos, and projects are processed and stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your personal media on any external servers.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Our app processes the following data locally on your device:

  • Photos and Images: Images you select from your device gallery to create slideshows
  • Music Files: Audio files you choose from your device or our built-in music library
  • Project Data: Slideshow configurations, settings, and preferences

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, certain information may be collected automatically:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction patterns, and crash reports
  • Advertising Data: Information collected by our advertising partners (see Advertising section)

Permissions We Request

Our app requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Storage/Media Access: To read photos and music files from your device and save exported videos
  • Internet Access: Required for displaying advertisements and checking for app updates

We only request permissions that are essential for the app's core functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our app's functionality
  • To improve and optimize user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To analyze app usage and fix bugs
  • To respond to user inquiries and support requests

Advertising

Our app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads help us keep the app free for users. Google AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized advertisements.

Information that may be collected by AdMob includes:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device and browser information
  • Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

Your photos, videos, and project files are stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of data.

Local Processing: All video rendering and image processing occurs entirely on your device. Your media files are never uploaded to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Google Play Services: For app functionality and updates

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access all your data directly within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your projects and exported videos at any time
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored app data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
