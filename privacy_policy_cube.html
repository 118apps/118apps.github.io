Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Magic Cube ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, session duration
  • Advertising Data: Ad interactions and identifiers for advertising purposes

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or phone data
  • Photos or media files

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Improve and optimize the App experience
  • Display personalized advertisements
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Analytics for Firebase

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

Google Play Billing

For in-app purchases, we use Google Play Billing. Purchase transactions are processed by Google Play, and we do not store payment information.

Advertising

Our App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests and usage patterns. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

To opt out on Android:

  • Open Settings
  • Go to Google > Ads
  • Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

In-App Purchases

Our App offers in-app purchases to remove advertisements. These purchases are processed through Google Play, and we do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Retention

We retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Analytics data is retained according to Google's data retention policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through our App. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
