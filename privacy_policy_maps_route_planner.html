Privacy Policy for Maps and Route Planner

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Maps and Route Planner ("we," "our," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

By using Maps and Route Planner, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

Why We Collect It:

  • To provide core GPS navigation and location tracking features
  • To display your current location on the map
  • To calculate routes and provide turn-by-turn directions
  • To show nearby places and points of interest
  • To provide traffic information for your area
  • To enable distance measurement and speed tracking
  • To optimize route planning

How We Use It:

  • Location data is processed locally on your device
  • Location information is shared with Google Maps API to render maps and calculate routes
  • We do not store your location history on our servers
  • Location data is only used while the app is active and you have granted permission

2. Device Information

We may collect basic device information including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Device language settings
  • Device screen resolution

This information helps us optimize the app's performance and troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app:

  • Features you use most frequently
  • App crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics

This data is aggregated and anonymized, and cannot be used to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. To Provide Core Services:

  • Display maps and your current location
  • Calculate and display navigation routes
  • Show traffic conditions
  • Display weather information
  • Provide compass and speedometer functionality
  • Enable route optimization
  • Show nearby places and points of interest

2. To Improve the App:

  • Analyze app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand feature usage to prioritize improvements
  • Optimize user experience

3. To Display Advertisements:

  • Show relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Ads help us keep the app free for all users

Google Maps Platform

  • Purpose: Map display, route calculation, place search

  • Privacy Policy: Google Maps Privacy Policy

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information
  • For Service Provision: With Google Maps API to provide navigation services
  • For Advertising: With Google AdMob to display ads
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our legal rights

Permissions Required

Required Permissions

Location Permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION):

  • Essential for GPS navigation and location-based features
  • The app can still be used without this permission, but GPS functionality will be limited
  • You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings

Internet Permission:

  • Required to load maps, traffic data, and weather information
  • Necessary for displaying advertisements

Optional Permissions

Notification Permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS):

  • Allows the app to send you push notifications
  • You can use the app without granting this permission
  • No notifications will be sent if you deny this permission
  • Can be changed anytime in your device settings

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Location data is processed locally on your device whenever possible
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all network communications
  • We do not store your personal information or location history on our servers
  • Third-party services we use employ industry-standard security measures

Data Retention

  • Location Data: Not stored permanently; only used during active navigation sessions
  • Usage Analytics: Retained in aggregated, anonymized form
  • Crash Logs: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we have about you
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • Permission Control: You can revoke location or notification permissions at any time through your device settings

How to Manage Your Privacy

To Disable Location Tracking:

Go to your device Settings → Apps → Maps and Route Planner → Permissions → Location → Deny

To Opt Out of Personalized Ads:

Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

To Manage Notifications:

Go to your device Settings → Apps → Maps and Route Planner → Notifications → Disable

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.
