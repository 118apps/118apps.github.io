Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Bible Verses ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our App is designed to function with minimal data collection:

  • Local Data: Your favorite verses and app preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for analytics and improving our services.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to personalize ads. This includes:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Google Analytics for Firebase

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This helps us improve the user experience. Firebase may collect:

  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Performance data

For more information, visit: Firebase Privacy Policy

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our App
  • Improve and optimize our App
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your data

  • The right to opt-out of personalized advertising

To opt out of personalized ads, you can adjust your device settings or visit Google Ads Settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
