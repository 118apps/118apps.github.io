Privacy Policy

Gallery Lock - Your Privacy, Our Priority

Effective Date: November 28, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Gallery Lock. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Our Core Principle: Your private files stay private. All encryption and decryption happens locally on your device. We never have access to your hidden photos, videos, or documents.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

  • Your PIN code (stored securely using Android Keystore encryption)
  • Security question and answer (encrypted locally)
  • Hidden media files (photos, videos, documents) encrypted with AES-256
  • App lock preferences and locked app list
  • App settings and preferences

Important: This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Anonymous crash reports and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience
  • Device information (model, OS version) for compatibility purposes
  • Advertising ID for personalized ads (can be reset in device settings)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide secure vault functionality for your private files
  • To protect your selected apps with PIN/fingerprint lock
  • To improve app stability and fix bugs
  • To understand usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements

Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data:

  • All hidden files are encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Encryption keys are stored in Android Keystore (hardware-backed on supported devices)
  • PIN codes are securely hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Biometric data is processed by your device's secure enclave, never by our app
  • No cloud storage - all sensitive data remains on your device

Zero-Knowledge Architecture: We have designed Gallery Lock so that we cannot access your hidden content even if we wanted to. Your privacy is mathematically guaranteed.

Third-Party Services

Gallery Lock uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We display advertisements to support free access to our app. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics

We use Firebase services to collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. This helps us improve app performance and fix issues quickly. No personal or sensitive data is shared with Firebase.

For more information about how Google handles data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Permissions We Request

  • Storage Access: Required to hide and encrypt your photos, videos, and documents
  • Camera (Optional): Only if you choose to take photos directly within the app
  • Biometric: To enable fingerprint/face unlock for added convenience
  • Usage Access: Required for App Lock feature to detect when protected apps are opened
  • Display Over Apps: Required to show the lock screen when protected apps are launched
  • Notifications: To show App Lock service status

Data Retention

Your hidden files and settings are stored on your device until you choose to delete them or uninstall the app.

  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all hidden files and settings
  • Anonymous analytics data is retained in accordance with Firebase Analytics data retention policies
  • Crash reports are retained in accordance with Firebase Crashlytics data retention policies

Warning: If you forget your PIN and cannot answer your security question, there is no way to recover your hidden files. We do not have access to your encryption keys.

Children's Privacy

Gallery Lock is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data stored within the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app or using the restore function
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings
  • Disable analytics collection (contact us for instructions)
  • Request information about data we may have collected

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Effective Date" above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
