Privacy Policy

Black & White Photo
Last Updated: December 4, 2025

Welcome to Black & White Photo. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Our app is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's what you should know:

  • Photos: We access photos only when you explicitly select them for editing. Photos are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Camera: If you choose to take a photo within the app, we access your camera only during that action.
  • Storage: We save your edited creations to your device's gallery only when you choose to export them.
  • Device Information: We may collect anonymous device information (device type, OS version) for app improvement and crash reporting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our monochrome and black & white photo effects
  • Process your photos locally to apply timeless B&W filters
  • Save your creations when you choose to export them
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Display relevant advertisements

3. Advertising

Our app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads help us keep the app free. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized advertising. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at Google Privacy Policy.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings or visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your photos and creations are stored only on your device. We do not upload, store, or have access to your personal photos on any external server. All image processing happens locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Google Play Services: For app functionality
  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your saved creations from your device at any time
  • Deny camera or storage permissions
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request information about data we may have collected

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
