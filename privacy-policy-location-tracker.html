Introduction

Welcome to Location Tracker & Travel Tools ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

This Privacy Policy applies to the Location Tracker & GPS Tools application (package: com.appmaker.locationtracker) available on Google Play Store.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

Our App collects location data to provide core functionality:

  • Precise Location: GPS coordinates for location tracking, compass, speedometer, and navigation features
  • Background Location: When tracking is enabled, we collect location data even when the app is closed or not in use
  • Location History: Stored locally on your device for trip tracking and history features

Important: Location data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (for analytics and advertising)
  • App version

3. Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our App:

  • Features used
  • App crashes and errors
  • Session duration
  • User preferences

4. Advertising Data

Our App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These services may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Interaction with advertisements

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  1. Provide Core Services: Enable GPS tracking, compass, speedometer, weather, and navigation features
  2. Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality and user experience
  3. Display Advertisements: Show relevant ads to support free access to the App
  4. Technical Support: Diagnose and fix technical issues
