Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Video Editor No Watermark ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Our app is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Media Files: The app accesses photos and videos on your device solely for editing purposes. These files are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to any external servers.
  • Storage Access: We request storage permissions to save your edited videos and images to your device.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (device model, OS version) for app optimization and crash reporting.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide video and photo editing functionality
  • To save edited content to your device
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To display advertisements (through third-party ad networks)

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Google Analytics: For app analytics and crash reporting.

Data Storage and Security

  • All video and image processing is performed locally on your device.
  • We do not upload your media files to any external servers.
  • Edited files are saved to your device's storage.
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect any collected data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time to stop all data collection

Permissions We Request

  • Storage/Media Access: To read and save videos and images
  • Camera (optional): To record videos directly within the app
  • Internet: To display advertisements
