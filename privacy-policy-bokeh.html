Welcome to Bokeh Effect Overlay ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Key Point: Your photos are processed entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or have access to any of your photos.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Bokeh Effect Overlay is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, certain information may be collected automatically:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for advertising purposes

When you grant permission, the app accesses your device's camera and photo library to:

  • Load photos for editing
  • Save edited photos to your gallery
  • Capture new photos using the camera

Important: All photo processing occurs locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To improve and optimize the app's performance
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs
